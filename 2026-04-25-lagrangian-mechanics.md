## What is Markdown?

Instead of writing `<h2>What is Markdown?</h2>`, you just write `## What is Markdown?`. Here are the basics you'll use most:

- **Bold text**: wrap with `**double asterisks**`
- *Italic text*: wrap with `*single asterisks*`
- A bullet list: start lines with `-`
- A [link](https://example.com): write `[link text](url)`
